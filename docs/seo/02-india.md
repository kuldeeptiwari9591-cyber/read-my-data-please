# 02 — India Keyword Strategy

India is the **#1 traffic source** for PDF tools globally. iLovePDF alone pulls **47.4M organic visits/mo** from India (`semrush:in`). Smallpdf gets 9.4M, PDF24 1.6M. The market is ~5–10× larger than the US for the same keywords.

## Why India is different

1. **Volumes are massive.** `compress pdf` IN = 2.24M/mo (vs 110K US). `merge pdf` IN = 3.35M/mo (vs 135K US).
2. **Government file-size limits drive intent.** UPSC, SSC, IBPS, state exam portals reject files above 100KB, 200KB, 500KB. That creates exact-size search niches that don't exist in the West.
3. **Mobile-first.** "mobile-in" Semrush database shows iLovePDF gets 45M/mo from IN mobile. Optimise everything for one-handed phone use.
4. **Cheap CPC, fierce SEO competition.** CPC is ~$0.03 (vs $0.40 US) but KDI on head terms stays 80+. Quick-wins live in long-tail + Hinglish.
5. **Free is the price.** Indian users will not pay $9/mo for Smallpdf — every conversion KW carries "free" intent.
